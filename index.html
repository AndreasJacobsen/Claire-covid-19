AI researchers support efforts to combat COVID19

Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies have successfully been applied in China, Taiwan, Singapore and South Korea in their efforts against COVID19. To help replicate this success in Europe and elsewhere, as of 30 March 2020, 95 AI and related laboratories from CLAIRE have volunteered to help apply AI to the pandemic crisis faced by all our societies. Here you can find our task force charter and press release.

You can click here to see a list of existing initiatives (projects, funding calls, hackathons, open datasets or tools, etc) by which AI and emerging technologies are contributing to combat COVID19.
